Vietnam - Timeless Charm, Modern Spirit

Vietnam, officially The Socialist Republic of Vietnam, is a country in Southeast Asia. Located on the eastern edge of the Indochinese Peninsula, it is bordered by China to the north, Laos and Cambodia to the west, and the South China Sea to the east. Vietnam is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, delicious cuisine, and stunning natural beauty, including beaches, mountains, and rivers.

Vietnam - A Destination Unlike Any Other

Vietnam geography is diverse, ranging from towering mountains in the north to fertile deltas in the south, with a long coastline of over 3,400 kilometers. Vietnam has a population of over 99 million people, making it the 15th most populous country in the world. The Kinh (Viet) people constitute the majority, but Vietnam is also home to over 50 ethnic minority groups, each with its own distinct culture and language. Many ethnic minorities live in the mountainous regions.
